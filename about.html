Who We Are

About Whez Global International Limited (WGIL) Whez Global International Limited (WGIL) is a reputable indigenous company providing professional services in Construction, Electrification & Renewable Energy, Procurement, and Logistics. The company is committed to delivering innovative, cost-effective, and sustainable solutions that meet the needs of both public and private sector clients. WGIL has successfully executed several construction and procurement contracts, earning a reputation for quality workmanship, timely project delivery, and exceptional customer satisfaction. Our strength lies in our team of highly qualified and experienced professionals, supported by a competent management team with extensive industry expertise. At WGIL, we are driven by excellence, integrity, safety, and innovation, ensuring that every project is delivered to the highest standards while creating lasting value for our clients and stakeholders.
